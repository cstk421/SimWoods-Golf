## Recording Tips

1. **Resolution:** Record at 1920x1080 minimum
2. **Audio:** Use a good microphone, record in quiet room
3. **Cursor:** Use cursor highlighter software for visibility
4. **Speed:** Move slowly, pause on important elements
5. **Length:** Keep under 5 minutes total
6. **Format:** Export as MP4 (H.264) for compatibility
